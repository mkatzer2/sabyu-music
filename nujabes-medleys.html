Nujabes Medleys

These medleys are a tribute to the one and only, Jun Seba aka Nujabes. I wanted to create a medley for each season that would capture the beauty of both the music of Nujabes and the scenery of the PNW! Both the spring and summer medleys have completed tracks as well as music videos and the fall medley has a video coming soon! Thanks for checking it out and spread the love!
