Navigator EP

A lone traveler, his canoe, and his guitar. With guidance from Polaris, the north star, he leaves his island home in search of a new one. He paddles out and races toward his new adventure. He paddles to the end of the ocean and realizes there is much more that lies beyond. He launches into space, admiring the beauty before him and in the distance he sees a planet - a planet the color of flametrees. He rows toward the planet and touches down. He explores the wondrous life on this skyland and understands where he his. He has made his voyage home.

He has become the Navigator.
